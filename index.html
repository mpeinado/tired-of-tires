Who we Are

Our mission is to raise awareness about local issues surrounding mass waste in Lake County. Tired of Tires has been active in Lake County since 2016. To date, we have focused on removing old car tires and appliances that have been improperly dumped in Lake Countyâ€™s forests using a bike and bike trailer. Through a partnership with the Lake County Landfill, Tired of Tires was able to dispose of the tires free of charge. Tired of Tires would not exist without the support of the Lake County community and specifically the Lake County Landfill and Lake County Board of County Commissioners.
